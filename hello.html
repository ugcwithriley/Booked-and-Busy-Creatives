Our Journey

Founded in 2025, Booked & Busy Createives began because there was a gap in the market: more and more people were finally betting on themselves and chasing their dreams. However the funds to keep on chasing their dreams were running thin, and that's where B&BC comes in to save the day and your pockets .

Our founder, Riley Diamond wants people to fully become who they want to be and not who they are forced to be, she understands that sometimes situations hinder us from reaching our highest high. B&BC is working hard on ensureing all their clients get what they paid for, get them paid. In addition, we are committed to ensuring that we enhance everyone's skills so that they can prosper beyond their time in B&BC.

Our Morals and Values

At Booked and Busy Creatives, we pride ourselves in creating a positive, creative and educational envrionment that helps you get creative and financial freedom. Our values include:

  • Quality Equipment: We use only high-quality equipment to help edify the skills, mindset and overall quality of the performance of the B&BC team.
  • Creativity: We adore helping and watching our students, clients and the brands that we work with to respectfully explore their creativity in all posssible aspects,as we don't want to limit anyone.
  • Attentive and Motivated: We pay close attention to the progress of the students in order to find out where they might need improvement and or motivation to better. Our coaches work hand in hand with the students to ensure they get the help they need.
  • Organized: We are committed ensuring that our creative space of work is also one that is organized, effective, safe and conducive to work in so that we don't encounter any issues as we work.
